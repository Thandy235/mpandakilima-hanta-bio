Mpandakilima Derby Kaindu Hante is a multi-disciplinary creative force reshaping business media and education in Zambia. At 27, he brings a rare combination of technical mastery and artistic vision to his role as Copperbelt Regional Manager for Financial Insight Zambia.

Born in Mufulira and now based in Kitwe, Hante's journey through Zambia's creative industries spans over a decade. What began at age 14 as a passion for graphic design evolved into a comprehensive creative career encompassing videography, photography, content direction, and strategic media management.

Educational Foundation

Mpandakilima Derby Hante's academic journey reflects a commitment to continuous learning and multidisciplinary excellence. He completed his O Level education with distinction, achieving 6 distinctions in his Grade 12 certificate, a testament to his early dedication to academic excellence.

Recognizing the power of visual communication in modern media, Hante pursued professional training in graphic design, equipping himself with creative skills that would later prove invaluable in his media management career. Currently, he is pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering, demonstrating his analytical mindset and understanding of infrastructure development, critical knowledge for covering Zambia's construction, mining, and industrial sectors.

Complementing his technical education, Hante is also pursuing certification as an Investment Advisor and Stockbroker, deepening his expertise in capital markets and financial services. This combination of creative, technical, and financial knowledge uniquely positions him to translate complex business concepts into accessible content for Financial Insight's diverse audience, from C-Suite executives to aspiring entrepreneurs across the Copperbelt Province and beyond.

This extensive foundation positions him as one of the Copperbelt's most versatile media professionals. Before joining Financial Insight, Hante served as Creative Director and Lead Designer at Bytedance's LetsChat - Zambia, where he shaped visual communication strategies for one of the region's emerging tech platforms.

He also held the position of Media Director for the Copperbelt Permanent Secretary, where he managed high-stakes government communications and refined his ability to translate complex policy into accessible public messaging.

Today, as Financial Insight's regional face in Kitwe, Hante wears multiple hats: photographer capturing Zambia's business landscape, videographer producing compelling visual narratives, on-camera host for educational content including the popular "2 Minutes with Hante" series, and strategic manager growing FI's Copperbelt presence.

His work demystifies business concepts, from market capitalization to supply chain management, for audiences ranging from C-suite executives to aspiring entrepreneurs, always grounding abstract theories in tangible Zambian examples.

Whether he's editing a 2-minute business explainer, directing a documentary-style Founders Series episode, composing a track in the studio, or hosting the FIZ Show, the through-line remains constant: storytelling that resonates.

His tagline, "The Huntsman," reflects this relentless pursuit of narratives that matter, tracking down the stories, the angles, the moments that reveal deeper truths about Zambia's evolving business landscape.

Hante represents a new generation of Zambian media professionals: technically skilled, creatively fearless, and deeply committed to making business intelligence not just accessible, but genuinely engaging for the audiences who need it most.
